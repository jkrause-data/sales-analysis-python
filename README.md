# Sales Analysis 2019

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from an electronics retailer using Python and exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques.

The objective was to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, seasonal patterns, and product performance. The analysis includes data cleaning, feature engineering, descriptive statistics, data visualization, and time series analysis.

The project was completed as part of a Data Analytics training program and has been further refined for portfolio purposes.

---

## Business Questions

The analysis focuses on the following questions:

1. How did sales develop throughout the year?
2. Which products generated the highest revenue?
3. Which products were sold most frequently?
4. How are order values distributed?
5. Are there differences in sales performance across weekdays?
6. At what times of day are customers most active?
7. Can seasonal patterns be identified through time series analysis?

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains sales transactions from an electronics retailer during 2019.

The original dataset was provided as part of a training project and is therefore not included in this repository.

Key attributes include:

- Order ID
- Product
- Quantity Ordered

- Order Date

## Key Findings

### Seasonality

Sales activity increased significantly during the final quarter of the year, with December representing the strongest sales period.

### Product Performance

Accessories such as batteries and charging cables achieved the highest sales volumes.

Revenue was primarily driven by premium products such as:

- MacBook Pro Laptop
- iPhone
- ThinkPad Laptop
- Google Phone

### Customer Behavior

Customer purchasing activity showed:

- Stable revenue distribution across weekdays
- Higher order activity around midday
- Increased purchasing activity during early evening hours

### Order Values

Most orders were concentrated in lower price ranges, while a smaller number of high-value purchases contributed disproportionately to total revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

- Focus marketing campaigns on the final quarter of the year.
- Leverage seasonal events such as Black Friday and holiday shopping periods.
- Use bundle promotions to combine accessories with premium products.
- Schedule advertising during peak purchasing hours.
- Maintain continuous campaigns throughout the week rather than concentrating on specific weekdays.
